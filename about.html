Our Story

Alubonets Self-Help Group was founded in 2012. The idea for the group arose during a pivotal moment of family need that brought several family members together to offer their collective support. This gathering highlighted that many relatives did not know one another or where other family members lived. This experience inspired the family to establish a group through which members could meet regularly, support one another, and strengthen their family relationships.

The Meaning Behind Our Name

The name 'Alubonets' is a proud tribute to the descendants of the late Alubokho. It serves as a living symbol of our shared identity, heritage, and the family lineage that connects the various branches of the wider Alubokho family across generations.
